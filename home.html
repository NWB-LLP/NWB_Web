About Us

What We Aim

  • Our Values
  • Our Vision
  • Our Mission

At Network Without Borders (NWB), we operate with an aim to provide advanced solutions to various real-world problems. We pledge to apply the following ideals in our work: Our values express essential concepts that we seek to make central to our work (both externally and internally), we revisit our ideals as a group on a regular basis, making changes as needed and we follow principles like The Golden Circle and The IDEA Framework.

In venturing a vision of technological possibilities in education rather than simply projecting linear or exponential changes in performance, we critically thinks not only of how technical improvements lead to the substitutions of new generation of tools for existing ones, but also of how entirely new uses, and perhaps new needs, may emerge. We envision a community in which all students receive the high-quality, egalitarian, and engaging education they require to reach their unique potential.

Expanding on the company's tagline, "Connect, Innovate, Shine," our mission is to network with various businesses and technologies from around the world to develop innovative solutions; connect educators and learners with the knowledge, tools, and networks they require to efficaciously transform education.
